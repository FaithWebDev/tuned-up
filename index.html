Tune Up, Tune Out.

Have you ever felt overwhelmed by the stressors in life? Like you needed a way to escape? Well, that's why we're here. Music is a way for everyone to tune out the chaos of life and enjoy the moment. Everyone is welcome at our establishment whether you are buying something or just need a safe, calm place to be. So if you need a place to Tune Up, and Tune Out, then come on by. We look forward to seeing you!

What is tuned up?

Tuned up is your one stop shop for all things music. We have all kinds of instruments from guitars, drums, and basses, to violins, pianos, and flutes. You need it? We got it. If we don't have it? We'll get it!

Us + Our Community

Community is very special to everyone at Tuned Up. We are locals, so we're very close to the culture and people here. Every other week we host a "Kids Who Rock" class at the community center. This is a program for children that can't afford typical one on one lessons to come and learn to play an instrument, or just be part of something. If you're interested in being part of this program or programs to come, click below to find out how you can help.

Our Services

By now you know what we're all about at Tuned Up. Aside from community outreach and selling instruments we also have some services you may be interested in. See below for more information.

Instrument Repairs

G string break again? Broke your drumstick smashing the drums a bit too hard? Little brother spill milk on your electric piano? Don't fret. Tuned Up has you covered. We employ highly skilled techs that will fix you up in a jiffy and for a fair price!
