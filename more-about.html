Get to know us

Our Mission:

  • To cultivate a welcome environment for people of all ages. We want everyone to feel involved in music.

    To be a business someone could look up to and regard as an example of how to treat their customers and how to run a business with respect and honesty.

    To bring the highest quality gear, at the best prices possible, to our community.

Founders + Where We Started

Tuned Up was founded in 2013 by Patrick Greene and myself, Liz Carter. Patrick and I grew up together in the city. We came from a place where people weren't very well off and struggled to make ends meet. Growing up, we would always "jam" together at least once a week. It was always our time of peace amidst the chaos of the outside world. With Tuned Up, we wanted to bring that piece of peace to others in the community. We are a shop, a business, but we are community driven first and foremost.

Our outreach program has gotten many kids and young adults into learning music. It's given them a creative outlet that they may not have had otherwise. It brings us joy to know that we can make a difference in these young individual's lives.
